Why Egghead?

Are you an Egghead? Do you want to be one? If you answered yes, start exercising your brain with Egghead! Based on real TV questions from the popular game show Jeopardy!

The questions are from a dataset of over 200,000 real questions collected between 1984 and 2012. You'll never get bored and always have a new question to think about!

The dataset was scraped from publicly contributed content on here by fans. Jeopardy! is a registered trademark of Jeopardy Productions, Inc.
